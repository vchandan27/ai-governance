Product perspective

How to improve this draft

The tracker should mature from keyword triage to traceable, auditable governance intelligence.

Policy intelligence

Add semantic clause extraction, obligation tagging, version comparison, policy-owner workflows, and confidence scoring with reviewer overrides.

Framework depth

Expand controls into citations, jurisdictions, applicability triggers, evidence requirements, and obligation-to-control mappings.

Enterprise workflow

Track remediation plans, owners, due dates, approvals, audit logs, exceptions, and integration with GRC, ticketing, DMS, and model inventory systems.

Assurance and safety

Add human review gates, source citations, red-team checks, privacy-preserving processing, role-based access, and exportable auditor-ready evidence packs.

Draft only. Consult qualified legal, compliance, and risk professionals before making governance decisions.
